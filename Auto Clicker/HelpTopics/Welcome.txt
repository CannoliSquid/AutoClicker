Welcome to the SkillerAgent Auto Clicker!

This application assists with tasks that require a lot of repetitive clicking. This can be especially helpful with certain video games that may require a lot of repetitions of tedious clicking actions.

This tool allows the user to continue these repetitive actions and make progress, while allowing them to shift their attention and focus to other matters (most effectively, away-from-keyboard activities).

Please keep in mind that, if using this tool with a game, it may be against Terms of service (ToS) or the End User License Agreement (EULA) and you may risk getting banned from the game.
